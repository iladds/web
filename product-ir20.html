ILADDS Laser System ILADDS IR20 Radar

ILADDS IR20 Low-Altitude Surveillance Radar

Tactical 3D Radar for Superior Situational Awareness

The ILADDS IR20 is a tactical 3D radar system designed to provide superior situational awareness and force protection in complex operational environments. It delivers high-precision target location and motion data, enabling accurate threat assessment, effective weapon cueing, and successful engagement.

With 360° continuous coverage from -5° to 60° elevation and the ability to simultaneously detect and track over 500 air, ground, and sea-based threats, the IR20 ensures effectiveness against saturation attacks and multi-thread environments. Its high sub-clutter visibility allows detection and tracking of low-flying drones and cruise missiles even against heavy ground clutter, urban canyons, and adverse weather.

Key Military Advantages

Superior Battlefield Situational Awareness & Targeting Accuracy

Delivers high-precision target location and motion data (Range: ≤5m, Velocity: ≤0.5m/s, Angle: ≤0.3°), enabling accurate threat assessment, effective weapon cueing, and successful engagement.

Comprehensive Battlespace Surveillance & Saturation Response

Provides 360° continuous coverage from -5° to 60° elevation. It can simultaneously detect and track over 500 air, ground, and sea-based threats, ensuring effectiveness against saturation attacks and in multi-thread environments.

High-Intensity Maneuver Warfare Tracking

With a rapid refresh rate of up to 360°/s, the system maintains positive and precise tracks on high-speed, maneuvering threats like low-flying aircraft, helicopters, and fast-attack craft, ensuring continuous fire control quality data.

LPI & Enhanced Battlefield Survivability

Utilizing low-power Continuous Wave technology, the radar has a very low probability of intercept, making it difficult for enemy ESM systems to detect. This prevents the platform from being targeted by anti-radiation missiles, ensuring mission longevity.

Exceptional Performance in Cluttered & Urban Terrain

High sub-clutter visibility allows for the detection and tracking of low-flying drones and cruise missiles even against heavy ground clutter, urban canyons, and adverse weather, providing a critical defense against terrain-masked threats.

Strategic & Tactical Mobility

Lightweight (≤55kg main unit + pedestal) and modular design enables rapid deployment and redeployment by small teams. Can be swiftly transported and mounted on various tactical vehicles for high-mobility operations.

High Availability & Mission Assurance

Operates reliably in extreme conditions (-40°C to +55°C) with an IP66/67 rating. Low power consumption (≤400W) simplifies integration with tactical power sources.

Integrated Air Defense Network Node

Serves as a high-performance sensor for C2 nodes, capable of cuing EO/IR systems and jammers for hard/soft-kill interdiction, making it ideal for point defense, forward operating base protection, and counter-UAS missions.
